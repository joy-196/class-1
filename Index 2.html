Description


Vivo V23 5G Smartphone (8/128GB)


The Vivo V23 5G has a 6.44-inch Full HD+ AMOLED screen. It has a front camera with a medium notch. The rear camera has a triple 64+8+2 MP resolution, PDAF, ultrawide, OIS, macro lens, LED flash, and other features, as well as 4K video recording. The front camera has a dual 50+8 MP resolution. The Vivo V23 5G features a 4200 mAh battery with 44W Fast Charging. It has 8 GB of RAM, an octa-core CPU that can run at up to 2.5 GHz, and a Mali-G68 MC4 GPU. The MediaTek Dimensity 920 5G (6 nm) chipset powers it. The device has 128 GB of internal storage and no MicroSD card slot. This phone has an in-display (optical) fingerprint sensor

Mediatek Dimensity 920 Processor


The Vivo V23 5G is powered by MediaTek Dimensity 920 octa-core 6nm processor. The Dimensity 920 boosts its Arm Cortex-A78 CPU frequencies up to 2.5GHz, offering exceptional performance for snappier App response, faster FPS in games, and better, connected experiences. With Dual-Mode 5G, feel the enhanced performance.


8GB RAM + 4GB Extended RAM


The Vivo V23 5G comes equipped with 8GB of RAM and it also supports virtual memory extension. Up to 4GB of your idle ROM space can be used as extended RAM to make switching between apps even smoother.


50MP AF Dual Selfie


Vivo V23 5G smartphone comes with a dual camera setup for the best quality selfies. It has a 50MP AF main front camera and a 105° Super Wide-Angle Camera. It also has a Dual-Tone Spotlight for taking selfies in the dark. 50MP JNV with ISOCELL 3.0 (pixel isolation technology) gives you stunning details and colors in every photo
